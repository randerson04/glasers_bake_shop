About Us



Opened on April 2nd 1902 by John Herbert Glaser, mainly for the production of bread. The orignal set up was for a 24/7 production. The bakers worked in shifts to meet the local demand for various types of bread and baked goods. In about the mid 1920's came the infamous Black and White Cookie which became a New York City staple, even seen in an episode of Seinfeld. Another thing that Glaser's is known for is their Chocolate Layer Cake. They have remained in the same store front since that day in 1902 on 1st Avenue between 87th and 88th street. The bake shop is now owned by the thrid generation of Glaser.


Come in for some excellent baked goods or a Black and White, we hope to see you soon!
